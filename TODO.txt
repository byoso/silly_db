- some shortcuts for single table requests:
    db.create(table, fields=None or list, values=list) -> Create a new item directly in the db
    db.get(table, condition="", fields="*") -> Selection[DbItem...]
    db.id_get(table, id=None) -> DbItem


- DbItem have an attribute db_table, and some shortcuts too:
    DbItem.set("attribute" or [attributes, ...], value or [values, ...]) -> update in the DB
    DbItem.get("attribute")
    DbItem.delete()
